# Evidence

## Types

- There are currently seven unique pieces of evidence in the game that can be used to identify a ghost:
- :Get a level 5 reading on the EMF Reader
- :Witness a ghostly disturbance within D.O.T.S Projector lights
- :Uncover finger, hand, or footprints with the UV Light
- :Get a reading below 0°C/32°F on a Thermometer
- :See floating orbs on video (through Video Camera, Video Feed, or Head Gear)
- :Have a ghost write in a Ghost Writing Book that's been placed down
- :Hear a reply on a Spirit Box after asking the ghost a question

## Evidence Pages

### D.O.T.S. Projector

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/D.O.T.S._Projector

- If the favourite room is small, consider placing the D.O.T.S Projector in a neighbouring room to aid visibility
- The Projector emits a substantial amount of light when placed. Investigators can use them for illumination, especially with a spare Projector or when D.O.T.S evidence can be safely crossed off. Note that the D.O.T.S projector light does not mitigate sanity drain
- The Tier II and III D.O.T.S Projectors can be placed on some ceilings in a few maps, which may allow the light to cover a room with much more consistency. This is especially useful for the tier III projector, whose sweeping motions and cone-shaped light area can cause the ghost to potentially leave its light prematurely depending on the ghost's and the projector's movement
- The green light emitted may obscure nearby fingerprints or footprints; look carefully or remove the projector when checking for ultraviolet evidence
- The tier III D.O.T.S Projector emits a constant motor whirring sound when active, which can both be quite annoying and interfere with the player's hearing when trying to listen for ghost activity. If you press Interact when pointing at the projector, it will stop in its current position, allowing it to operate in complete silence at the cost of covering a smaller area; interacting again will turn it off completely, and interacting a third time will turn it back on again

### D.O.T.S. Projector (Evidence)

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/D.O.T.S._Projector_(Evidence)

- Ghosts with D.O.T.S evidence will occasionally enter a "D.O.T.S state", that lasts a maximum of 6 seconds, when trying to enter a roaming state. During the D.O.T.S state, a faint silhouette of the ghost will be visible only when it passes through a D.O.T.S. Projector's laser grid. The ghost will also:
- walk towards a random waypoint within the favourite room if there is no player inside the investigation area
- walk towards a random waypoint within 2 metres if there is a player inside the investigation area
- walk towards the nearest player if there is one in the same room as it
- While the silhouettes are visible to the naked eye (unless the ghost is a Goryo), a video camera with night vision may allow for better visibility. This also means that D.O.T.S can be tracked simultaneously alongside Ghost Orbs
- Dead players cannot see the D.O.T.S state by any means
- The Banshee has a 2/3 chance to walk towards its targeted player if they are inside the investigation area
- The Goryo will not enter a D.O.T.S state if there is a player in the same room as it, and its silhouette will not be visible to the naked eye

### EMF Level 5

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/EMF_Level_5

- When a ghost with EMF Level 5 as one of its evidences interacts with the environment, each EMF reading will have a 33% chance to be an EMF 5 emission instead of EMF 2 or EMF 3, which can be detected and confirmed with an EMF Reader. This applies to any action that will normally create EMF 2 or EMF 3, such as interactions, (some) abilities and so on. It does not apply to EMF 4
- Recording the sound of an EMF Level 5 will yield an EMF 5 Reading sound in the journal, rewarding up to $12 and 12 XP, depending on the tier of the Sound Recorder and whether there is a Unique stamp
- EMF 5 is a random chance, so one would want to increase the chance of the ghost interacting with the environment. One can place some props in the ghost room for the ghost to throw. Make sure to turn off the room lights, as ghosts cannot throw items if the lights in its current room are on. Smudging the ghost or saying recognised phrases near the ghost can mildly increase the chance/rate of interactions
- It is possible to "guess" EMF Level 5 as an evidence using the site activity monitor, though this is not guaranteed. This is because the site activity monitor is an approximate representation of the total EMF currently present in the map, with a modifier of 0, -1 or -2 for each EMF generated. Hence, EMF level 5 can be "guessed" if multiple straight-line jumps of 4 and 5 levels are observed, especially if no players are currently inside the investigation area. Though an EMF 5 emission can be represented by an activity change of 3 levels, an EMF 3 is just as likely to do the same, so a jump of 3 levels is generally ignored
- Some occurrences may also show up as jumps of 4 or 5 levels; avoid erroneously taking these to represent EMF 5:
- A ghost event, which generates EMF 4. Hunts also always generate EMF 4 but this is rarely observed
- The Poltergeist's multi-throw ability
- Using cursed possessions to force interactions or ghost events, resulting in two EMF 2/3 interactions happening within the same second, or EMF 4 from ghost events and hunts

### Freezing Temperatures

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/Freezing_Temperatures

- The presence of a ghost in a room will cause the room's temperature to decrease, regardless of evidence. Ghosts without Freezing Temperatures evidence will be able to drop the room's temperature to , whereas ghosts with Freezing Temperatures as evidence will be able to drop the temperature to 
- Freezing Temperatures can only be confirmed if the thermometer reads a temperature strictly below 
- If using a Tier I thermometer, Freezing Temperatures should only be confirmed if the thermometer reads a temperature strictly below as it is less accurate between and (there are only 8 graduations between these two values instead of 9)
- Taking a photo of the thermometer while it is displaying a freezing temperature will yield a Freezing Temperatures photo, rewarding up to $7 and 7 XP, depending on the tier of the Photo Camera
- Temperatures only decrease when the ghost is within a room, and although the ghost is more likely to stay in its ghost room, making it colder, freezing temperatures do not necessarily indicate the ghost room
- Additionally, temperatures do not return to normal immediately after a ghost leaves a room. This means that multiple rooms can be freezing at once, and that players should not assume that low or freezing temperatures guarantees that the ghost is still in that room
- Since 0.9.0, seeing cold breath is not an indicator of Freezing Temperatures
- If the fuse box is turned off at the start, Freezing Temperatures may be obtained quicker, depending on the weather and how often the ghost roams into other rooms. In particular, one could theoretically observe Freezing Temperatures in approximately two minutes if it is snowing, as long as the ghost stays in the same room for long enough

### Ghost Orb

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost_Orb

- When a map is loaded in, Ghost Orbs will spawn in the favourite room and float within the room. They look like very small orbs that glide across the screen, generally one at a time. They can be seen through the screen on a Video Camera with night vision, or through any available video feed with night vision, but not through night vision goggles (Tier III Head Gear)
- Depending on the map and the location of the favourite room, Ghost Orbs can sometimes be seen via the map's CCTVs if the CCTV happens to be facing into the favourite room, though this is usually rare. Ghost Orbs will periodically shift position within the favourite room, forcing players to be more proactive in locating them
- When the ghost room changes on difficulties where 'Changing favourite room' is not set to 'None' (such as on Professional, Nightmare, or Insanity), the Ghost Orbs will also move to the new favourite room
- Filming a Ghost Orb will yield a Ghost Orbs video in the journal, rewarding up to $12 and 12 XP, depending on the tier of the video camera and whether there is a Unique stamp

### Ghost Room

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost_Room

- Whenever a map is loaded in, one room in the area will be randomly selected as the favourite room. The favourite room is relevant for the following behaviours:
- The ghost will teleport to the favourite room at the end of every hunt and when a crucifix has successfully prevented a hunt
- The ghost can roam back to the favourite room
- Ghost Orbs, if the ghost has it as evidence, will be present in the favourite room
- Though The Mimic doesn't have Ghost Orb among its types of evidence, it will still provide it as its weakness
- The Haunted Mirror will show a view of the favourite room
- The Hermit tarot card and the "I wish to trap the ghost" wish from the Monkey Paw will teleport the ghost to the favourite room and temporarily trap it there
- The Yurei will be sent back to the favourite room and stay within it for 90 seconds after being affected with Incense outside of a hunt

### Ghost Writing

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/Ghost_Writing

- If Ghost Writing is a piece of evidence, the ghost will try to write in an opened Ghost Writing Book every 45 to 90 seconds and the chance to succeed depends on the ghost writing book tier: 51%, 67%, 76% for tiers 1, 2, 3, respectively. The timer starts as soon as the equipment is loaded and is different for each book
- Ghost Writing cannot be given if the Ghost Writing Book is closed and/or the ghost is outside of its range. However, if the interaction succeeds, the book will rise and the pencil/pen will start to frantically scribble on the pages visually and audibly. An EMF Level 2 reading will also be created (33% chance of being an EMF Level 5 reading instead if the ghost has EMF Level 5 as evidence)
- There are several different types of Ghost Writing that a ghost can put in the book, with the tier I and tier III books both having three, and the tier II book having six. Each has an equal chance of occurring and the type of writing does not narrow down the type of ghost any more than the fact that the ghost writes in the book at all. Each book may contain different types of writing
- Taking a photo of the Ghost Writing Book after it has been written in will yield a photo labelled as Ghost Writing, and reward up to $7 and 7 XP, depending on the tier of the Photo Camera and whether there is a Unique stamp. A video and a sound labelled as Ghost Writing, each awarding up to $12 and 12 XP, can also be recorded when the ghost is writing in the book
- If a ghost throws a Ghost Writing Book, then it either does not have Ghost Writing as evidence, or is hiding it on reduced-evidence difficulties

### Spirit Box (Evidence)

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/Spirit_Box_(Evidence)

- In order to get a successful Spirit Box response from the ghost, all of the following is required:
- The ghost has Spirit Box as one of its types of evidence
- The player must be in the same room as the ghost
- The player must be within a certain range of the ghost (3 meters with a Tier I, 4 meters with a Tier II, and 5 meters with a Tier III)
- All lights in said room must be off. Light emitted from equipment, including electronic and non-electronic equipment, does not affect this. Lights in other rooms, including rooms or hallways that are directly adjacent, may be left on, and any doors between the rooms can stay open
- Ghosts which respond only to "people who are alone" will respond only if there is a single person in the room at the moment the question is asked. If the ghost is noted to respond to everyone, the number of people in the room does not affect the ghost's ability to respond. If the difficulty has a setup time (e.g. Amateur and Intermediate), this will be indicated on the cork board and in the journal
- If all other conditions are met, there is a chance of the ghost responding per question asked. The chance depends on the tier of the Spirit Box: 50%, 60%, 70% for tiers 1, 2, 3, respectively
- If the player is particularly unlucky, a combination of these factors may cause the Spirit Box to repeatedly turn the LEDs red, or show an 'X', despite the ghost having Spirit Box as one of its evidences

### Ultraviolet

Source: https://phasmophobia.fandom.com/wiki/Ultraviolet

- Ultraviolet evidence consists of fingerprints and footprints which can be revealed by using a UV Light
- By default, each interaction on a valid surface will always produce a fingerprint. Exceptions include with the Obake which has a 25% chance to hide them, or when using the "Fingerprint chance" custom difficulty modifier to reduce the odds
- A ghost with Ultraviolet evidence will leave behind fingerprints when it interacts with the following surfaces or items:
- Most types of doors
- Light switches and lamps
- Mirrors (specific maps only)
- The Prison map has additional places where fingerprints may be left behind:
- On the horizontal surface of a bar of cell doors
