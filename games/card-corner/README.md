# Card Corner

**Card Corner** is an idle poker game that runs in a small window in the corner of your screen while you work, browse, or play other games. Build your hand, collect loot, craft gear, and rebirth to grow stronger across runs.

Developed by **Conradical Games** and published by **Assemble Entertainment GmbH**.

[Card Corner on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4081590/Card_Corner/)
