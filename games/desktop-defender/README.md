# Desktop Defender

**Desktop Defender** is an idle auto-battler that runs in a small window in the corner of your screen while you work, browse, or play other games. You defend a lone tower against endless enemy waves, level up, collect loot, craft gear, and ascend to grow stronger across runs.

Developed by **Conradical Games** and published by **Poysky Productions**.

[Desktop Defender on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3772810/Desktop_Defender/)
